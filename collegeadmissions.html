College Admissions Counselling

Our counselors know what it takes to make it through the college application process – every member on our team has been accepted to the most prestigious institutions – Harvard, Yale, Princeton, Stanford, UPenn, Duke, Rice – in the last two years. Our experienced counselors get to know our students beyond their grades and test scores – what makes them tick and where they want to go – so that we can craft an individualized plan to help them gain admission to the right university. We offer help no matter where you are in your high school experience. In fact, in the modern competitive environment, the earlier, the better.

We offer the following services (flexible based your need):

  • Review of personal student and parent questionnaires and transcript
  • Exploring and planning for PSAT, SAT, ACT, SAT Subject Tests, and AP exams
  • Strategically planning high school coursework, ensuring a balance of challenging courses
  • Assessment of your particular high school's resources and how best to make use of them
  • Selecting extracurricular activities that you enjoy and taking leadership in them to the next level
  • Considering summer plans, including internships, part-time jobs, pre-college programs, and research opportunities
  • Assistance with applications to contests, scholarships, and prestigious awards
  • Identifying teachers and mentors for recommendations and developing good rapport with them

For juniors and seniors nearing application season, we also offer the following services:

  • Review of high school coursework, transcripts, and standardized test scores
  • Review of extracurricular activities, interests, and other significant commitments
  • Identifying teacher and mentors and asking for letters of recommendations
  • Developing a list of prospective colleges – a combination of safety, target, and reach schools – after in-depth discussions about your desires and goals
  • Assistance with junior and senior year course selection
  • Discussion of various Early Action/Early Decision options and how to apply "early" to a college
  • Careful review of admission requirements at each college to which you are applying
  • Development of timetable for completing application components
  • Complete essay support – assistance with ideas development, review and editing of multiple essay drafts, and detailed feedback that improves writing quality and presentation of your personality.
  • Interview guidance
  • Assistance with navigating being "deferred" in a college's early applicant pool.
  • Assistance with navigating being placed on a college's waitlist.
  • Development of art portfolios and supplemental documents

As always, the Top College Approach means that you will be working with the best of the best. All of our tutors scored a 2300+ or higher on the SAT and attend one of the top universities in the country. We also understand high school students often times have busy schedules. We’ll work to create a plan that works around your schedule and helps you succeed.
