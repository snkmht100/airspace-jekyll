BS/MD

Students who want to pursue a career in medicine may find the best option is a BS/MD program, where they are able to pursue their interests while having the assurance of being accepted to medical school and becoming a doctor. Admit rates to B.S./M.D. programs are often lower than getting into an Ivy League school. Our counselors have been accepted to some of the top BS/MD programs – Northwestern HPME, Brown PLME, Rice/Baylor – and are incredibly familiar with the BS/MD admission pathway including extracurricular activities, volunteering, research, essays, and interview prep.

AP Tests/Subject Tutoring

As college admissions continue to get more and more competitive, students seek more ways to differentiate themselves from the competition.
